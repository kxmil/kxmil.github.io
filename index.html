Philosophy

Ego has no place in the design process. Fail quickly to succeed quickly. Negative feedback is a gift. Universal design is ethical design. Designers are toolmakers. Meet people where they are. Be a facilitator. Define the problem. Play. There's always more than one solution. Empathy is the key to resonance. You are not your user.
