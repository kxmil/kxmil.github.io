overview

prompt

HR teams today often work on time-consuming tasks such as sourcing via social networks or constructing skills maps, taking time away from more value-add tasks such as relationship building and recruitment strategy.
The search for CVs also concerns sales representatives who have to compile dossiers in response to tenders. However, this task often has to be carried out in urgently owing to the response deadlines requested.
In the digital age, turning to data science solutions is a necessary approach to improving human resource management.

Initial Design Thinking

How might we create Emoby's user interface to ensure a smooth and efficient experience for HR professionals searching for candidate profiles?

How might we integrate additional data sources and enhance the semantic analysis to offer even more accurate and relevant search results for HR teams?

How might we provide personalized insights and recommendations for career development and mobility to individual employees, thereby addressing their unique needs and aspirations?

problem statement

Our problem statement revolves around enabling Emoby to offer a user-centric, data-driven solution that enhances the efficiency and accuracy of talent acquisition, ensuring HR professionals can make informed decisions and meet their recruitment needs effectively.

production

wireframing

In the wireframing phase for Emoby, we meticulously crafted an intuitive interface for seamlessly retrieving data from various sources, ensuring that the system could access diverse CV databases with ease. We also implemented a robust profile matching interface, leveraging advanced algorithms to precisely align candidate profiles with specific job descriptions and skills, thereby simplifying the talent acquisition process.


To further enhance user experience, we developed a sophisticated mechanism for HR professionals to efficiently shortlist CVs, streamlining the selection process and saving valuable time while ensuring that only the most qualified candidates were considered.


user testing

We built a PoC based on wireframe to be able to test it with the users. Tests data report for Emoby reveals essential insights for the platform's development. The tests aimed to gather feedback to enhance the matching algorithm and gather user feedback to align Emoby more closely with user needs. The sample included various profiles, from managers to recruitment specialists, reflecting the diverse user base. The feedback and modification suggestions provided valuable input for refining the user interface and interaction design. Data from the tests also showed promising statistics, indicating strong user engagement, making Emoby a promising solution in the field of talent acquisition.

development


Emoby's design journey reached its final stages with a concerted effort to create a user-centric and highly efficient platform. After collecting valuable feedback from users, we were determined to integrate their insights and needs into the design.



Through collaborative co-creation workshops, we engaged directly with users, enabling them to actively participate in shaping the platform's features and functionality. This iterative process ensured that Emoby would be aligned with the precise requirements of its user base.

visual identity



The visual identity of the interface received special attention. We applied a cohesive and visually appealing identity to the tool, ensuring it not only performs exceptionally but also provides a pleasant and engaging experience. The combination of thoughtful feature implementation, user feedback integration, and a distinctive visual identity culminated in the completion of the Emoby design, ready to offer an exceptional solution in the realm of talent acquisition

reflections

outcome

In 6 months, we built a technology capable of converting candidate CV databases into searchable semantic spaces in a matter of seconds.

This allows human resources professionals to:
• Search for profiles in a database of CVs supported by artificial intelligence from a need formulated in a job description
• Automatically generate skill and job maps
